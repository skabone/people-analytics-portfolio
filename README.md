## Projects

### [01 — IBM HR Attrition Analysis](./01_IBM_HR_Attrition_Analysis/)
**Tools:** Python, Scikit-learn, Pandas · **Domain:** Predictive Workforce Analytics

Logistic regression, Decision Tree, and Random Forest models predicting voluntary employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics dataset (1,470 employees, 35 features). Addresses class imbalance via SMOTE and ADASYN. Logistic Regression achieves AUC = 0.934. Findings grounded in Job Demands-Resources model, Organizational Embeddedness Theory, and Equity Theory.

---

### [02 — Project Bid Accuracy Analysis (NDA Org)](./02_NDA_Org_Bid_Accuracy_Analysis_R/)
**Tools:** R, RMarkdown, OLS Regression · **Domain:** Consulting Analytics / Organizational Research

*NDA engagement — all client and personnel identifiers anonymized.*

Ten OLS regression models identifying structural drivers of bid-to-invoice discrepancy at a mid-size public safety assessment firm (N = 279 project records, FY2020–2021). Key finding: consultant identity and four specific client organizations predict estimation error; industry sector and assessed rank do not.

---

### [03 — Data Scientist Salary Market Analysis (2021)](./03_DataScientist_Salary_PowerBI/)
**Tools:** Python, RapidMiner, Power BI · **Domain:** Labor Market Analytics / Workforce Intelligence

Exploratory analysis of 742 Glassdoor-scraped data science job postings. Examines salary by geography, tool demand by role type, and organizational profile of DS-hiring firms. Python dominates across all role types; coastal markets show disproportionate volume and pay; Key Influencers visual reveals tool profiles for DS, DA, and Data Engineer roles.

---

### [04 — America's Broadband Problem](./04_US_Broadband_Tableau/)
**Tools:** Python, RapidMiner, Tableau · **Domain:** Public Policy Analytics / Equity Research

County-level analysis of U.S. broadband access inequality (N = 3,102 counties) using merged IMLS, BroadbandNow, and FCC data. Finds that poverty rate predicts actual broadband access better than official FCC metrics; identifies geographic gradients and disparate impact on Black American communities. [View interactive Tableau dashboard →](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/mintay/viz/DVProject1-Tableau-AmericasBroadbandProblem/AmericasBroadbandProblem)
